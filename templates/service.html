Web Application Penetration Testing

Our comprehensive web application penetration testing identifies vulnerabilities in your web applications before attackers can exploit them.

Modern web applications are complex and often contain vulnerabilities that can lead to data breaches, unauthorized access, or system compromise. Our expert team uses both automated tools and manual testing techniques to thoroughly assess your web applications for security flaws.

We follow industry-standard methodologies including OWASP Top 10 and SANS Top 25 to identify critical vulnerabilities such as:

  • SQL Injection and other code injection vulnerabilities
  • Cross-Site Scripting (XSS) and Cross-Site Request Forgery (CSRF)
  • Authentication and session management flaws
  • Insecure direct object references and access control issues
  • Security misconfiguration and sensitive data exposure

After testing, we provide a comprehensive report with detailed findings, risk assessments, and actionable remediation recommendations prioritized by severity.

Testing Methodology

We employ a structured approach including reconnaissance, mapping, vulnerability discovery, exploitation, and post-exploitation analysis.

Coverage Areas

Front-end interfaces, APIs, database connections, authentication mechanisms, and business logic vulnerabilities.

Deliverables

Executive summary, detailed technical findings, proof-of-concept demonstrations, and prioritized remediation recommendations.

Benefits

Reduced risk of data breaches, improved compliance posture, and enhanced security awareness among development teams.

Phishing Awareness and Training

Our phishing awareness and training program strengthens your human firewall by educating employees on recognizing and responding to phishing attempts.

Vulnerability Assessment

Our comprehensive vulnerability assessment service identifies, categorizes, and prioritizes security weaknesses across your IT infrastructure.

Vulnerabilities in your systems and applications can provide attackers with entry points into your network. Our vulnerability assessment service helps you identify and address these weaknesses before they can be exploited, strengthening your overall security posture.

Our vulnerability assessment service includes:

  • Comprehensive scanning of internal and external systems
  • Identification of security misconfigurations
  • Detection of missing patches and outdated software
  • Identification of weak authentication and encryption implementations
  • Validation and elimination of false positives
  • Prioritization of findings based on risk level

Unlike basic automated scanning, our vulnerability assessment combines automated tools with manual verification and analysis to provide accurate results with actionable remediation recommendations.
