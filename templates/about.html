Our Story

Founded in 2023, Kogabe emerged from a vision to create a cyber security company that combines technical excellence with a client-focused approach.

Our founders, with decades of combined experience in information security, recognized the need for a security partner that understood both the technical complexities of cyber threats and the business realities organizations face.

Today, Kogabe has grown into a trusted security partner for companies across industries, helping them navigate the increasingly complex digital threat landscape with confidence.

Based in Madurai, Tamil Nadu, we serve clients throughout India and beyond, bringing world-class security solutions to organizations of all sizes.
